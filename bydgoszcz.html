🇵🇱 The Trip

The story

The Polish Christmas market city you've never heard of — but need to visit.

"Bydgoszcz is one of those places that you stumble upon and immediately wonder why more people are not talking about it. Beautiful, festive, friendly and wonderfully good value — it is Christmas market magic without the tourist crowds."

We headed to Bydgoszcz in December 2024 with a friend — and from the moment we arrived, the city had us. While everyone else was heading to Kraków or Warsaw for the Christmas markets, we had stumbled on something a little more special — a city that felt entirely its own, entirely authentic and entirely brilliant.

Friday evening was spent exactly as it should be — glühwein in hand, wandering the market stalls as the city glittered around us. The Christmas lights in Bydgoszcz are genuinely stunning — the blue arches and illuminated Mill Island along the Brda river create an atmosphere that is hard to put into words.

Saturday was a full day of exploring — the markets, the beautiful waterfront, the Old Town and Mill Island, which is one of the most picturesque spots we have come across anywhere in Poland. We wined and dined brilliantly, spending very little by Western European standards.

The highlight of the whole trip? Meeting some locals who took a shine to us and proceeded to show us the best of Bydgoszcz's nightlife — spots we would never have found on our own. That kind of spontaneous, warm hospitality is something you cannot plan and cannot put a price on. It made the trip genuinely unforgettable.

We flew home on Sunday already talking about coming back. Bydgoszcz — you are very much on our list.

Common questions

Bydgoszcz FAQ

Is Bydgoszcz worth visiting for the Christmas markets?
Absolutely — and it is one of Poland's most underrated Christmas market destinations. Unlike the more well-known Polish cities, Bydgoszcz feels authentic and unhurried. The markets are beautiful, the lights are stunning and the atmosphere is genuinely magical without the crowds you get elsewhere.
Where is Bydgoszcz and how do you get there?
Bydgoszcz is located in north-central Poland and has its own international airport with direct flights from several European cities including Dublin. It is also well connected by train to Warsaw and other major Polish cities, making it easy to include as part of a wider Poland trip.
What is Bydgoszcz like?
Bydgoszcz is a beautiful, vibrant city with a stunning waterfront along the Brda river, a charming Old Town and the famous Mill Island — a picturesque island in the middle of the river that is especially magical at Christmas. The city has a brilliant food and drink scene and the locals are incredibly warm and welcoming.
Is Bydgoszcz good value for money?
Excellent value — like most Polish cities, Bydgoszcz is very affordable compared to Western Europe. Food, drinks and accommodation are all great value, which makes it a brilliant destination for a budget-friendly Christmas break.
How many days do you need in Bydgoszcz?
A weekend of two to three nights is perfect — it gives you time to explore the Christmas markets properly, wander the Old Town and Mill Island, enjoy the food and nightlife and still feel like you have seen the best of the city without rushing.
Are the people in Bydgoszcz friendly?
Incredibly friendly — we were lucky enough to meet some locals who took us under their wing and showed us the best of the city's nightspots. That kind of warmth and hospitality is something you do not always find as a tourist, and it made the trip truly special.
