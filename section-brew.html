What's the best gluten-free friendly café in Carlow or Kilkenny?

The Wandering Donkey (Carlow), The Wandering Elk (Portlaoise), Stradbally Fayre (Laois), and Tower and Bean (Timahoe) each earn a full 5-star coffee rating with confirmed gluten-free treat options as of July 2026. We personally taste-test the coffee, atmosphere, service, and — because one of us is coeliac — the gluten-free options at every café we review, from small-town Ireland to a medina café in Marrakech.
