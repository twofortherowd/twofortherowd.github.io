What is TourRadar?

What is TourRadar?

TourRadar is an online marketplace that lets you compare thousands of organised tours from hundreds of operators all over the world. Instead of booking directly with dozens of individual companies, TourRadar lets you compare itineraries, prices, inclusions, reviews and availability — all in one place.

For destinations like Morocco, Egypt, Costa Rica and Madagascar, that saves hours of research.

Why we chose it

Why we chose TourRadar for Morocco

Normally we organise every trip ourselves. Morocco felt different. We wanted someone else to organise hotels, transport between cities, the desert camp, local guides, and all the logistics in between.

Instead of spending weeks planning, everything was organised before we even landed.

Pros and cons

Pros and cons of booking through TourRadar

✅ Pros

  • Huge choice of tours
  • Genuine customer reviews
  • Easy comparison between operators
  • Secure online booking
  • Lots of departure dates
  • Excellent for countries where organising transport yourself is more difficult

⚠️ Cons

  • Always read exactly what's included
  • Flights usually aren't included
  • Some tours have optional extras
  • Prices can change depending on departure date
  • Nobody warns you about the altitude on the Tizi n'Tichka pass (2,000m+) — expect a headache for a few hours, and the Sahara camp gets properly cold at night once the sun drops, so pack a warm layer even if you've spent the day sweating in Marrakech
