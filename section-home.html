Two for the Row'd — the real story

Fáilte! Nicola agus Pádraig is ainm dúinn agus is Éireannaigh muid! Táimid inár gconaí i gCeatharlach agus is aoibhinn linn bheith ag taisteal. Táimid tar éis 64 tír a fheiceáil agus níl sé i gceist againn stopadh! 🇮🇪

We're Nicola and Padraig — a husband and wife team from Carlow with a serious case of wanderlust and absolutely no intention of slowing down. Between us we've visited 64 countries and we're only getting started.

Nicola is a French teacher by day, a rock DJ on local radio by night, and a full-time travel obsessive in between. Podge works in the bank, runs his own podcast, and is equally as mad about getting on a plane to somewhere new. We keep it useful, funny, and real — no generic waffle, no influencer fluff. Just places, trips, cafés, hotels and adventures we've actually had and can properly talk about.

Our audience follows along because it feels like travelling with us — not being sold to from a distance. We're delighted to be official TourRadar Ambassadors and proud affiliates of GetYourGuide, Klook and Amazon — everything we recommend is something we've genuinely used, booked, or packed ourselves. 🇮🇪
