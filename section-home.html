Two for the Row'd — the real story

We're Nicola and Pádraig — a husband and wife team from Carlow with a serious case of wanderlust and absolutely no intention of slowing down. Between us we've visited 60+ countries & territories and we're only getting started.

Nicola is a French teacher, a fully accredited tour guide and a full-time travel obsessive. Podge works in the bank, runs his own podcast and is equally as mad about getting on a plane somewhere new. We keep it useful, funny and real — no waffle, no influencer fluff. Just places, trips, cafés, hotels and adventures we've actually had.

Our audience follows along because it feels like travelling with us — not being sold to from a distance. 🇮🇪
