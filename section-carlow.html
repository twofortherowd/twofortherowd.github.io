History of Carlow — Walt Disney's ancestors, Custer's Last Stand and 22 saints from one tiny county

We're Nicola and Pádraig and we're from Carlow. The history of Carlow is more extraordinary than most people realise — one of the smallest counties in Ireland, and one of the most historically remarkable. A doctor blew up the castle. A mansion burned with a banshee curse attached. The largest megalithic capstone in Europe is sitting in a field three kilometres from the town centre. We've made 18 short films to prove it.
