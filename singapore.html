The story

One night, one city — and it delivered everything

Singapore was a stopover on the way to Australia but we were absolutely not going to waste it. We hit the ground running — hiring a private guide for the day meant we covered the city properly without having to figure anything out ourselves. The Colonial District, Chinatown, Little India, the incredible skyline — Singapore has a density of things worth seeing that very few cities in the world can match.

The food was a revelation. Singapore's hawker centres are legendary for good reason — some of the best food we've ever had, at prices that make you double check the bill. Local dishes, unfamiliar flavours, and the kind of atmosphere that only happens when a whole city takes food this seriously.

Then came the highlight of the whole stopover — the Singapore Zoo night safari. Watching nocturnal animals in their natural setting after dark, with an extraordinary light show, is one of the most unique wildlife experiences we've ever had in 57 countries. It was genuinely spectacular and unlike anything else we've done.

And then karaoke. Because Singapore at night demands it. 🎤

One night. We'd go back for a week in a heartbeat.

Common questions

Singapore FAQ

Is Singapore worth visiting on a stopover?
Absolutely yes. Even one night in Singapore is enough to get a real taste of the city — the food alone is worth the stopover. Add a private city tour and the Singapore Zoo night safari and you have one of the best one-night stopover experiences anywhere in the world. We'd go back for a full week without hesitation.
Is the Singapore Zoo night safari worth it?
Yes — one of the most unique wildlife experiences we've ever had in 57 countries. Seeing nocturnal animals in their natural setting after dark, with a brilliant light show, is genuinely unlike anything else. It is one of the best things to do in Singapore and should be at the top of your list if you have even one evening there. Book in advance as it fills up fast.
What is the best thing to do in Singapore on a short stopover?
A private city tour is the most efficient way to see Singapore's highlights in a short time — the Colonial District, Chinatown, Little India, Marina Bay and the skyline. Combine it with the night safari at the zoo and as much local food as you can fit in and you have a brilliant stopover. Finish with karaoke for the full Singapore experience. 😄
What is the local food like in Singapore?
Extraordinary — Singapore has one of the best food scenes in the world. The hawker centres are legendary — incredibly good food at very reasonable prices. Laksa, chicken rice, chilli crab, roti prata and satay are all must-tries. Even a short stopover is more than enough to get a proper taste and understand why people rave about it.
How long do you need in Singapore?
Even one night gives you a brilliant taste. Two to three days lets you explore properly — the neighbourhoods, the food scene, Sentosa Island, Gardens by the Bay and the zoo. A week would give you time to really slow down and appreciate it fully. Whatever time you have — use every single minute of it.

Planning a Singapore stopover or trip?

Even one night is enough to fall in love with Singapore. Start with the zoo night safari and a city tour — and leave time for the food. Lots of food.
